THREAT_MODEL: DATA_HARVESTING

Data harvesting apps often use "cosmetic rewards" (like avatars or filters) to bypass user skepticism. Your location and contacts are high-value metadata used to map your social graph. Total denial (A) is the safest, but informed consent through research (C) is the professional standard.
